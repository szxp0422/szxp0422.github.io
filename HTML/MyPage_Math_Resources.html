Math Resources

Here are some cool links and interesting things to look at.

  • Art of Problem Solving (AOPS): https://artofproblemsolving.com/
    • Recommend checking out community for content (math and non-math) and For the Win for a fun competition.
  • Mock Tests for AMC on AOPS: https://artofproblemsolving.com/wiki/index.php/Mock_AMC
  • Mock Tests for AIME on AOPS: https://artofproblemsolving.com/wiki/index.php/Mock_AIME
  • Mock Tests for USAJMO on AOPS: https://artofproblemsolving.com/wiki/index.php/Mock_USAJMO
  • Mock Tests for USAMO on AOPS: https://artofproblemsolving.com/wiki/index.php/Mock_USAMO
    • Recommend doing problem sets by the Christmas Math Committee for high quality problems (slightly harder than usual though)
  • Random Problem Generator: https://amctrivial.com/
  • Wolfram Alpha: https://wolframalpha.com/
  • Desmos (for 2D graphing): https://www.desmos.com/
  • Geogebra (for diagrams and fancier graphing(2D and 3D)): https://www.geogebra.org/
