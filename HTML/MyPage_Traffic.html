Traffic Simulation Improvements

Abstract: This paper proposes an adaptive, non-cyclic traffic light system that ensures maximal throughput on small scale urban road junctions. This new method identifies the number of cars waiting at each street and decides when the traffic light should change. By providing a definitive threshold along with the weight of each street, the wait times for each car are kept to a minimum, with a maximum of 2 minutes. We specifically look at two adjacent road intersections, consisting of 5 source roads and 5 sink roads, in San Jose, California. In the end, we see a much higher throughput compared to the traditional method of cyclic traffic lights.
